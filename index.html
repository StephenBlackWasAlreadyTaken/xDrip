DexDrip

  • A wireless bluetooth bridge that lets your phone pick up readings from your dexcom transmitter
  • Custom algorithm that thus far seems to hold its own against dexcoms (and so far beat it!) ( Please note: I personally have only ever compared this to the adult version of the Dexcom Algorithm, therefore I cannot recommend it for anyone under the age of 18 )
  • No plugging things into your receiver or your phone, phone can be used as a normal phone!

NOW SYNCS TO NIGHTSCOUT THROUGH MONGO OR REST API!!!

Currently there are 2 parts

  • one is the Relay (the wixel and bluetooth component) which catches Dexcom transmissions them and then sends them out over BT

  • The second part is the Android App, this receives that data and graphs it out for you (and allows you to enter calibrations)

  • Both are available and ready for you to use!
