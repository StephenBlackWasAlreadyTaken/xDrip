DexDrip

DexDrip allows you to use your phone as a Dexcom Receiver (Wirelessly! through the magic of bluetooth!)

Currently there are 2 parts

  • one is the Relay (the wixel and bluetooth component) which catches Dexcom transmissions them and then sends them out over BT

  • The second part is the Android App, this receives that data and graphs it out for you (and allows you to enter calibrations)

  • Both are available and ready for you to use!
